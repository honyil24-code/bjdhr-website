个人简介 | Biography

孙七教授长期从事医疗健康信息化标准研究，主持制定国家标准10余项，参与国际标准制定3项。在健康数据安全与隐私保护方面有深入研究。

Prof. Sun Qi has long been engaged in medical health informatization standards research, leading the formulation of over 10 national standards and participating in 3 international standards. He has in-depth research on health data security and privacy protection.
