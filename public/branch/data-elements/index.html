Health Service Data Elements Branch

分会简介 | About the Branch

北京数字健康研究会健康服务数据要素分会成立于2026年2月8日，是研究会下设的专业分会，聚焦数据要素全链条关键环节。

The Health Service Data Elements Branch of BJDHR was established on February 8, 2026, as a specialized branch focusing on the entire data elements value chain.

成立背景 | Background

分会成立大会于2026年2月8日在京圆满召开，获国家卫健委及卫生发展研究中心、北京市卫健委等部门指导，中国社区卫生协会、北京大学临床研究所等众多机构专家，以及数据要素领域高校教授、行业领军企业高管等80余人参会。

The founding conference was held in Beijing on February 8, 2026, with guidance from the National Health Commission, Health Development Research Center, and Beijing Municipal Health Commission. Over 80 experts attended, including representatives from China Community Health Association, Peking University Clinical Research Institute, university professors, and industry leaders.

宗旨与工作目标 | Mission & Objectives

以推动健康医疗行业高质量发展为核心，重点开展五大工作：

  1. 制定医疗服务数据资产的行业标准 — 规范行业发展
  2. 服务地市改革 — 提供信息化规划与专业支撑
  3. 联动数据交易所 — 推动健康服务数据合规流通
  4. 依托国家部委课题 — “数据要素赋能医院高质量发展”，打造并引领地市实践案例
  5. 搭建高端交流平台 — 促进行业经验共享与资源联动

Focusing on high-quality development of the health industry, we carry out five key initiatives:

  1. Develop industry standards for medical service data assets
  2. Support municipal reforms with informatization planning
  3. Connect with data exchanges to promote compliant data circulation
  4. Lead national ministry projects on “Data Elements Empowering High-Quality Hospital Development”
  5. Build high-end exchange platforms for industry collaboration
