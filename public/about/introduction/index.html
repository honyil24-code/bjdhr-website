关于北京数字健康研究会 | About BJDHR

基本信息 | Basic Information

中文： 北京数字健康研究会（Beijing Digital Health Research Institute，简称BJDHR）成立于2022年，是由北京市卫生健康委员会主管的学术性社会团体。研究会致力于推动数字健康领域的学术研究、技术创新和产业应用，促进健康医疗大数据、人工智能、物联网等新技术在健康领域的深度融合与应用。

English: The Beijing Digital Health Research Institute (BJDHR), established in 2022, is an academic social organization supervised by the Beijing Municipal Health Commission. The Institute is dedicated to promoting academic research, technological innovation, and industrial applications in the field of digital health.

我们的使命 | Our Mission

  • 开展数字健康领域的学术交流、学术研究

  • 促进数字健康技术创新与成果转化

  • 提供专业培训、会议服务、咨询服务

  • 推动跨部门协作与产业合作

  • Conduct academic exchange and research in digital health

  • Promote technological innovation and transformation

  • Provide professional training, conference services, and consulting

  • Facilitate cross-sector collaboration and industrial cooperation
