研究会简介 | Institute Introduction

中文： 北京数字健康研究会（Beijing Digital Health Research Institute，简称BJDHR）成立于2024年，是由北京市卫生健康委员会主管的学术性社会团体。研究会致力于推动数字健康领域的学术研究、技术创新和产业应用，促进健康医疗大数据、人工智能、物联网等新技术在健康领域的深度融合与应用。

English: The Beijing Digital Health Research Institute (BJDHR), established in 2024, is an academic social organization supervised by the Beijing Municipal Health Commission. The Institute is dedicated to promoting academic research, technological innovation, and industrial applications in the field of digital health, facilitating the deep integration and application of new technologies such as health big data, artificial intelligence, and Internet of Things in healthcare.

我们的使命 | Our Mission

  • 推动数字健康领域的学术研究与交流

  • 促进数字健康技术创新与应用

  • 培养数字健康专业人才

  • 服务政府决策与产业发展

  • Promote academic research and exchange in digital health

  • Facilitate technological innovation and application

  • Cultivate professional talents in digital health

  • Serve government decision-making and industrial development
