政策研究 | Policy Research

中文： 研究会积极参与数字健康相关政策研究，为政府决策提供智力支持。主要研究方向包括：

  • 数字健康产业发展政策
  • 医疗数据治理政策
  • 互联网医疗监管政策
  • 人工智能医疗应用伦理规范

English: The Institute actively participates in digital health policy research, providing intellectual support for government decision-making. Main research directions include:

  • Digital health industry development policies
  • Medical data governance policies
  • Internet medical supervision policies
  • AI medical application ethics standards
