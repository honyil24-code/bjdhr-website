北京数字健康研究会正式成立 | BJDHR Officially Established

北京数字健康研究会正式成立 | BJDHR Officially Established

发布日期 | Date: 2026年4月10日 | April 10, 2026

中文：

今日，北京数字健康研究会（Beijing Digital Health Research Institute，简称BJDHR）正式成立。研究会由北京市卫生健康委员会主管，汇聚了来自北京大学、清华大学、北京航空航天大学、中国人民大学等高校，以及中国标准化研究院等科研机构的专家学者。

研究会将致力于：

  • 推动数字健康领域的学术研究与交流
  • 促进数字健康技术创新与应用
  • 培养数字健康专业人才
  • 服务政府决策与产业发展

欢迎广大数字健康领域的专家学者、企事业单位加入研究会，共同推动数字健康事业发展！

English:

Today, the Beijing Digital Health Research Institute (BJDHR) was officially established. Supervised by the Beijing Municipal Health Commission, the Institute brings together experts and scholars from Peking University, Tsinghua University, Beihang University, Renmin University of China, and the China National Institute of Standardization.

The Institute will be dedicated to:

  • Promoting academic research and exchange in digital health
  • Facilitating technological innovation and application
  • Cultivating professional talents in digital health
  • Serving government decision-making and industrial development

We welcome experts, scholars, enterprises and institutions in the digital health field to join the Institute and jointly promote the development of digital health!
