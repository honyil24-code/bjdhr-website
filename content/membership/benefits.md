## 会员权益 | Member Benefits

### 个人会员权益 | Individual Member Benefits

**中文：**
1. **学术交流** — 免费参加研究会组织的学术会议、研讨会
2. **信息发布** — 在研究会网站和公众号发布研究成果
3. **资源共享** — 获取研究会内部资料和研究报告
4. **培训机会** — 优先参加专业培训和继续教育
5. **荣誉评选** — 参与优秀会员、优秀论文等评选
6. **咨询服务** — 获得数字健康领域专业咨询

**English:**
1. **Academic Exchange** — Free participation in academic conferences and seminars
2. **Information Publishing** — Publish research results on the Institute's website and WeChat
3. **Resource Sharing** — Access to internal materials and research reports
4. **Training Opportunities** — Priority for professional training and continuing education
5. **Honor Selection** — Participate in outstanding member and paper selections
6. **Consultation Services** — Professional consultation in digital health

### 单位会员权益 | Institutional Member Benefits

- 单位负责人自动成为研究会理事
- 优先推荐申报政府项目
- 参与行业标准制定
- 技术成果优先转化推广
- 人才推荐与招聘服务

- Institutional leaders automatically become board members
- Priority recommendation for government projects
- Participation in industry standard formulation
- Priority promotion of technical achievements
- Talent recommendation and recruitment services
