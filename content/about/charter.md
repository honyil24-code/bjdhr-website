## 章程制度 | Charter & Regulations

### 北京数字健康研究会章程 | BJDHR Charter

**第一章 总则 | Chapter 1 General Provisions**

**第一条 | Article 1**
本会名称：北京数字健康研究会（英文名：Beijing Digital Health Research Institute，缩写：BJDHR）。

The name of this organization: Beijing Digital Health Research Institute (Abbreviation: BJDHR).

**第二条 | Article 2**
本会性质：由北京市从事数字健康相关领域的企事业单位、高等院校、科研机构和个人自愿组成的学术性、非营利性社会团体。

Nature: An academic, non-profit social organization voluntarily formed by enterprises, institutions, universities, research organizations and individuals engaged in digital health related fields in Beijing.

**第三条 | Article 3**
本会宗旨：遵守宪法、法律、法规和国家政策，践行社会主义核心价值观，推动数字健康领域学术研究与技术创新，促进数字健康产业发展。

Purpose: To abide by the Constitution, laws, regulations and national policies, practice core socialist values, promote academic research and technological innovation in digital health, and facilitate the development of the digital health industry.
