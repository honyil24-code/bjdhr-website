## 关于北京数字健康研究会 | About BJDHR

### 基本信息 | Basic Information

**中文：**
北京数字健康研究会（Beijing Digital Health Research Institute，简称BJDHR）成立于**2022年**，是由北京市卫生健康委员会主管的学术性社会团体。研究会致力于推动数字健康领域的学术研究、技术创新和产业应用，促进健康医疗大数据、人工智能、物联网等新技术在健康领域的深度融合与应用。

**English:**
The Beijing Digital Health Research Institute (BJDHR), established in **2022**, is an academic social organization supervised by the Beijing Municipal Health Commission. The Institute is dedicated to promoting academic research, technological innovation, and industrial applications in the field of digital health.

- 开展数字健康领域的学术交流、学术研究
