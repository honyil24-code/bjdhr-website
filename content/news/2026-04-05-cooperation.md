## 研究会与多家医院签署合作协议 | Cooperation Agreements Signed

**发布日期 | Date:** 2026年4月5日 | April 5, 2026

---

**中文：**

近日，北京数字健康研究会与北京大学第一医院、北京协和医院、中日友好医院等多家三甲医院签署战略合作协议，共同推进数字健康技术在临床的应用。

**合作内容包括：**
- 共建数字健康联合实验室
- 开展多中心临床研究
- 推进医疗人工智能产品临床验证
- 联合培养数字健康专业人才

此次合作标志着研究会在产学研协同创新方面迈出重要一步。

---

**English:**

Recently, BJDHR signed strategic cooperation agreements with several top-tier hospitals including Peking University First Hospital, Peking Union Medical College Hospital, and China-Japan Friendship Hospital to jointly promote the clinical application of digital health technologies.

**Cooperation areas include:**
- Building joint digital health laboratories
- Conducting multi-center clinical research
- Promoting clinical validation of medical AI products
- Jointly cultivating digital health talents

This cooperation marks an important step for the Institute in industry-university-research collaborative innovation.
